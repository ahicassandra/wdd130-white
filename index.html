Why Read Manga?

Unlike western comics, manga spans every genre imaginable—from intense sports dramas and high-stakes culinary battles to heartwarming slice-of-life stories and epic science fiction adventures.

Because creators (mangaka) have significant creative control, readers experience deeply personal, highly-serialized stories that have captivated millions around the world.
