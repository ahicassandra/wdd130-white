Unlocking the page: Right to Left

The very first hurdle for many newcomers is the format. Japanese manga is read from the top-right corner of the page down to the bottom-left corner. This matches the flow of traditional Japanese writing and preserves the original artistic vision of the creators. Don't worry if it feels unnatural for the first few pages! Within a single chapter, your brain will adapt to the visual rhythm.

Step-by-Step Panel Navigation:

  1. Start at the Top Right: Read the panel furthest to the right at the top of the page.
  2. Move Left: Read the panels horizontally to the left edge of the page.
  3. Drop Down: Once a horizontal row is complete, move down to the next row on the right side.
  4. Follow Dialogue Bubbles: Within an individual panel, read speech bubbles from right to left, top to bottom.
